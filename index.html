Aethra is building the ecosystem for the next generation of researchers and builders. We are a global network of students, researchers, and engineers working on the hardest problems in AI, climate, mathematics, and beyond. Through fellowships, open-source tools, hackathons, and community, we are creating the infrastructure that serious science needs.

Research

  • Research Fellowships Open

    Stipends and mentorship for independent researchers working on high-impact problems in AI, climate, and mathematics. Work on what you care about, with people who have cared about it longer.

  • Mentorship Network Open

    One-on-one matching with researchers and practitioners across fields and institutions.

  • Publication Support Open

    Editing, preprint reviews, and writing workshops to help researchers communicate their work clearly.

  • Research Essays Ongoing

    Longform essays on open problems written by researchers in the Aethra network. Published openly.

  • Open Problems Seminar Monthly

    Monthly sessions where researchers present unsolved questions they think deserve more attention.

  • Preprint Review Program Open

    Community peer review for preprints across AI alignment, optimization, climate systems, and computational biology.

Labs

What we believe

  • Research is a team sport.

    The most important discoveries happen at the intersection of disciplines, across geographies, between people who share obsessions but not assumptions.

  • Access is the bottleneck.

    Talent is uniformly distributed. Opportunity is not. The next great researcher might be sitting without a mentor, without funding, without a community that takes their questions seriously.

  • Tools shape thought.

    The instruments we build determine the questions we can ask. Better software, better infrastructure, and open models make better science possible.

  • The problems worth solving are hard.

    AI alignment, climate systems, the foundations of mathematics. These are not incremental improvements. They demand full intellectual commitment from the most serious people alive.

  • Community is infrastructure.

    A research community is not a nice-to-have. It is load-bearing. Aethra exists to build that infrastructure: permanent, open, and growing.

Roadmap

  • Phase 1 — Hackathons Complete

    Build the community through high-quality hackathons. Prove that serious, mission-driven events attract serious people. Establish the network.

  • Phase 2 — Ecosystem In Progress

    Launch Aethra Research, Aethra Labs, and full community infrastructure. Reach 10,000 students in the first wave. Grow to 100,000 in the second.

  • Phase 3 — Undeclared Future

    Something revolutionary. The kind of thing that only becomes possible once the ecosystem exists and the network is real.
