We believe in convergence.

I started Aethra because I kept noticing the same thing in my own research. The most interesting questions were never fully inside one field. My work in AI kept pulling me toward biology. My mathematics kept pulling me toward computation. The more I looked, the more I saw it everywhere: the best breakthroughs happen at the edges, where two disciplines collide and neither one has all the answers.

Convergence is what we call that collision. Aethra was built to create the conditions for it.

What convergence means

  • In research

    Working on a problem that genuinely requires two fields to solve it. Not just borrowing a tool. Actually needing both.

  • In events

    Putting a biologist, a software engineer, and a designer in the same room for 48 hours. Seeing what they build when no one tells them which lane to stay in.

  • In community

    A network where a question from one discipline gets answered by someone from a completely different one. That is where the interesting things happen.
