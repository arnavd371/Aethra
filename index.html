about
built by
students,
for students.

aethra is a fully student-run hackathon organization for teens ages 13 to 18. we run events where any student, regardless of skill level, gets to build something real.

in 2025 we ran 6 events with 1,704 participants and $12,520+ in prizes. now in 2026, moonshot is our biggest yet: $30,000+ in prizes across 11 categories. no-code builders stand alongside engineers here.

we believe the best ideas come from people who haven't been told it's impossible yet.
