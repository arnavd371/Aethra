Moonshot: A 0 to 1 Hackathon.
"The best way to predict the future is to invent it."

our seventh event and most ambitious yet. 11 prize categories across beginner friendly machine learning/AI and open-ended innovation tracks. moonshot was the first aethra event where top builders got directly connected to vetted research programs, fellowships, and mentors. the goal was to make what comes after the hackathon as valuable as the hackathon itself.
