Research

Aethra is not a general research organisation. We focus on interdisciplinary research specifically: work that genuinely requires more than one field to make progress. We think that is where the most important questions live.

What we support

  • Mentorship, publication, and peer review

    We provide mentorship connections, publication support, and a community of researchers who take hard questions seriously. Our current focus areas are AI safety, computational biology, climate systems, and the foundations of mathematics.

Open Problems Seminar

  • Open Problems Seminar Coming Soon

    Once a month, one unsolved problem at the intersection of two fields, and one researcher to present it. The goal is not to solve it in an hour. The goal is to make more people aware it exists.

Research Fellows

  • Research Fellows Coming Soon

    A small cohort of student researchers working on problems they chose themselves, with mentorship and community support from Aethra. Applications opening soon.
