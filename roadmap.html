Roadmap

Where Aethra is going.

  • Phase 1 — Hackathons Complete

    Build the community through high-quality hackathons. Prove that serious, mission-driven events attract serious people. Establish the network.

  • Phase 2 — Ecosystem In Progress

    Building the research layer on top of the community the hackathons created. Two things are in progress right now.

    Aethra Convergence Seminar Series. Once a month, one unsolved problem at the intersection of two fields, one researcher to present it. The goal is not to solve it in an hour. The goal is to make more people aware it exists.

    Aethra Interdisciplinary Journal. Peer-reviewed research that crosses field boundaries. If your work does not fit neatly inside one discipline, it probably belongs here. Submissions opening soon.

  • Phase 3 — Undeclared Future

    Something revolutionary. The kind of thing that only becomes possible once the ecosystem exists and the network is real.
